English

I am a third-year graduate student at the University of Utah. My advisors are Srikanth Iyengar and Rachel Skipper.

I earned my master's degree at CIMAT under the supervision of Pedro Luis del Ángel Rodríguez.

My research interests are commutative algebra, homological algebra, and representation theory.

I also co-organize the student Commutative Algebra Seminar (BIKES) at the University of Utah with Ethan Morgan.
